CLASSISM IN THE DESIGN DEGREE PROGRAMME
Who can study design?

Teamwork with: Luna Plecher

Includes: Zine A5 with 28 pages, smaller zine with 8 pages in easy language enclosed

It is my responsibility to design in a discrimination-critical and inclusive way.

Costs in studies, new purchases, aptitude tests and knowledge that is required. All of this excludes.
This zine explores the mechanisms of exclusion in a design degree programme and provides insights
into the social environments from which students come. At the end, it even dares to suggest solutions.
