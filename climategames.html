Climate Community Games

For this project I worked together with my fellow student Rania Steinicke. Our game Heat Survivors focuses on the needs of a variety of people, animals and plants in a neighborhood during a heat wave. It's about community and what we can do for others. Everyone can take simple steps to help the old neighbor, the crow in the park and the tree on the side of the road in the blazing sun. As a community, everyone gets through a heat wave well!
