Climate Community Games

Teamwork with: Rania Steinicke

Includes: 64 cards, packaging, instructions, hourglass

Climate education is important to me. That's why I worked on this topic both before and during my studies.

The game ‘Heat Survivors’ is a card game in which 3-6 players have to find sensible measures during
a heatwave in the city and then discuss them, focussing on information and self-efficacy. The game
encourages players to become active themselves in the area of heat protection.
