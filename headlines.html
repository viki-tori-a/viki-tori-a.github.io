VINYL-ARTWORKS - artworks for a fictional release-campange

Teamwork with: Stacey Rossner and Sarah Schneider

Includes: vinyl bag, snippet tape, single cover, labels

Teamwork requires compromises, but is also a lot of fun.

The music of the fictional band Solid States is composed like a patchwork of different sounds from different genres.
The artwork picks up on this aspect and is created from snippets sewn together.
