Kochbuch

Individual work

Includes: Cookbook A5 with 32 pages

Some topics need more attention.

The topic of water availability in connection with climate change is often not so present. The cookbook creates visibility by supplementing ten sustainable recipes from the Eberswalde University for Sustainable Development with infographics and tips on the topic of water consumption and saving water.
